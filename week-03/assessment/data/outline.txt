Solar Farm
Use Cases
As an administrator, I want to add a solar panel.

select add panel menu option
enter a section (string)
enter a row (int 1-250)
enter a column (int 1-250)
enter a material (select a material from a list...?)
enter year installed (int - present year or past)
tracking (yes/no)
application will save the panel
success or failure feedback


As an administrator, I want to delete a solar panel.
select delete panel menu
enter a section (panel key)
enter a row (panel key)
enter a column (panel key)
send panel key to app
success or failure feedback
As an administrator, I want to update a solar panel.

Option 1

select update panel menu
enter a section (panel key)
enter a row (panel key)
enter a column (panel key)
send panel key to app to fetch a panel
if the panel doesn't exist, give feedback
if the panel does exist, update fields:
enter a section (string) [allow ENTER to keep value]
enter a row (int 1-250)
enter a column (int 1-250)
enter a material (select a material from a list...?)
enter year installed (int - present year or past)
tracking (yes/no)
send updated panel to be saved by app
success or failure feedback


Option 2

select a update panel menu
enter a section
the app retrieves all panels in that section
user selects a panel to update (can display ids)
update fields:
enter a section (string) [allow ENTER to keep value]
enter a row (int 1-250)
enter a column (int 1-250)
enter a material (select a material from a list...?)
enter year installed (int - present year or past)
tracking (yes/no)
send updated panel to be saved by app
success or failure feedback


As an admin, I want to view panels by section.

select display by section menu
enter a section
send section criteria to app (find panels)
display panels
